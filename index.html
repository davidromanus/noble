Our backgrounds are diverse - we are founders, investors, operators of 9+ figure healthcare companies - all with the spirit of giving, connecting with our peers, and maximizing our collective impact.

Why we started Noble

We, as noble, seek to unify the physicians who want to chart the course of where our profession is headed. We are gathered to influence new business models, policy implications, and new how new technology should impact our practice.

Noble members want to turn the tide on where healthcare is headed,and we believe that stronger physician community is a prerequisite to making it happen. We believe that our relationships form the basis of our culture, and that culture is where change begins and ends.

We believe that if physicians lean into our profession's calling, we will lead the future of medicine. We dedicated our lives to serving patients. Society holds us accountable for patient health outcomes on an individual level, and it's our duty to evolve as indusrty leaders.

Our Model

We have seen the power of communnity, peer-groups, and coaching outside of medicine and thought - Why doesn't this exist for physicians?

We researched the most successful peer-group communities and took the best parts and designed Noble specifically for physicians.

Our core purpose centers around cultivating a physician-focused peer network, with a primary emphasis on 8-person core groups, ongoing knowledge-sharing among peers, and incredible person events.

  • Noble forum

    8 senior physician executives will join a personalized & curated forum for 7-8, confidential sessions facilitated by an executive coach. This forum will challenge your executives to grow professionally & keep each individual accountable.

  • In-Person Experiences

    Quarterly in-person experiences to foster long-term connections. Examples include dinner, lakedays, sporting events, and more

  • Digital Community

    Access to our highly engaged community of high-growth physicians and a dedicated concierge to help you connect with others in our community

  • Workshop Dialogues

    Exclusive sessions led by talented speakers who are responsive to Noble member needs and provide true insight into healthcare operations

Our vision is to build a community-driven ecosystem where the top physician leaders discover the nuances of disrupting healthcare, and accelerate their ideas, products, and services into solutions.

Who can apply?

Noble is currently only accepting applications for high-growth physician leaders. You must have over 2 years of experience leading teams in a clinical or healthcare setting, demonstrating strong leadership, innovation, and a commitment to driving positive outcomes. Ideal candidates should possess a proven track record of fostering team collaboration, implementing strategic initiatives, and achieving measurable improvements in patient care and operational efficiency.

To ensure the quality of our community remains high, each applicant is interviewed by our team. We are limiting the initial community to 100 members.
